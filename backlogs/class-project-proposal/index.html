Application Overview

Users are able to publish lecture videos for students to view. They are able to favorite lecture videos under certain classes, professors, and dates.

Technology Overview

The programming languages and resources used are HyperText Markup Language (HTML), Cascading Styling Sheets (CSS), JavaScript, React, Node.js, and Firebase.

Proposal Last Updated

April 7th, 2026

Feature List

  • Webpage with an ability to upload and store videos that can be filtered through classes, professors, and dates.
  • An aesthetically pleasing interface.
  • Ability to star/favorite lecture videos.
  • Download lecture videos.

Future Feature List

  • Accounts created through Google accounts.
  • Authentication with Google accounts.
  • Last recently viewed section.
